The Buenos Aires Chamber

Audience

My company especially focuses on young people between the ages of 14 and 25. It is a friendly website where people can enjoy learning online and it is important to emphasize the word online because most do not usually enjoy learning or taking online courses.
Young people will enjoy starting new projects alongside classmates and instructors in video conferencing such as group chats.
It will focus on young people who like to learn new hobbies as they prepare professionally for work in the labor market.

Scenario 1

How challenging for the user would be to navigate the site?

The page will have an easy user interface interaction that can smoothly be navigated.

Scenario 2

What are the advantages of becoming a member?

The agreement you have to accept before signing for, will demonstrate the benefits you can enjoy if you become a member.

Scenario 3

What is the layout you used to building this site?

The layout will be a grid columns and rows that can easily match with the purpose and the content of the page.

Scenario 4

How can you contact us?

There will be a formal contact information to get in touch with us.

Scenario 5

Is there another way to acces the site, for example in another viewport?

Yes, the site will be build in three points of view. For small and large devices.
