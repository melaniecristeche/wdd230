Purpose

Have you ever tried to learn new skills for work? I have a more fun question: Have you ever tried to find new hobbies but none of the YouTube videos interested you?
In my case, yes. I always tried to learn new job skills but all the course’s platforms I found were individual. Would you like to have a class with other people? If you don´t have friends, maybe with your family?
My company develops online courses that people use to learn new skills, find new jobs, or improve personal hobbies remotely. We additionally offer open classes that people can join and discuss the projects together as a group. This results in a multiple tool platform that connects people to reach knowledge.

Audience

My company especially focuses on young people between the ages of 14 and 25. It is a friendly website where people can enjoy learning online and it is important to emphasize the word online because most do not usually enjoy learning or taking online courses.
Young people will enjoy starting new projects alongside classmates and instructors in video conferencing such as group chats.
It will focus on young people who like to learn new hobbies as they prepare professionally for work in the labor market.
