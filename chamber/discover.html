Buenos Aires or the Autonomous City of Buenos Aires (CABA), is the capital city of the Argentine Republic, one of the thirteen nations that make up the South American subcontinent. It is one of the main capitals of the continent and the Western world, as well as an important tourist destination.
Buenos Aires is the largest city in Argentina, where most of the population and economic activity are concentrated. It is the political center of the nation, despite being constituted as a Federal Republic.

Its name comes from the cult of the Italian Madonna di Bonaira, widespread among the Iberian sailors of the time, whose name was Spanishized as Buen Ayre.
